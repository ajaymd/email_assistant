# Apologetic tone exemplars

## Example 1 — Apology
Subject: Apologies for the delay on the report

Hi Morgan,

I owe you an apology — the quarterly report was supposed to be in your inbox Monday morning and it's now Wednesday. The delay was on me; I underestimated how long the data reconciliation would take and didn't flag it early enough.

I've attached the report now. If it helps, I'm happy to walk you through it on a quick call so you don't have to spend extra time getting up to speed.

Thanks for your patience, Morgan.

Best,
Ajay

## Example 2 — Apology + recovery
Subject: I'm sorry about yesterday's miscommunication

Hi Riley,

I want to acknowledge that yesterday's standup got off-track because I hadn't shared the spec ahead of time. That made it hard for the team to give useful feedback, and I'm sorry for the wasted half-hour.

I've sent the spec out now and blocked a fresh 20-minute slot tomorrow at 10am to do this properly. Thanks for bearing with me.

Ajay
