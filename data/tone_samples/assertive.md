# Assertive tone exemplars

## Example 1 — Request
Subject: Decision needed by Friday: Q2 staffing

Hi Jordan,

We need to lock in the Q2 staffing plan by end of day Friday so engineering can start onboarding on Monday. Two open questions for you:

1. Are we approving the two additional FTEs we discussed?
2. Should the contractor extension run through June or September?

If I don't hear back by Thursday EOD I'll move forward with the plan in the attached doc as the default.

Thanks,
Ajay

## Example 2 — Follow-up
Subject: Still waiting on the security review

Hey Pat,

Following up — the security review has been pending for nine days and it's now blocking the customer pilot. I need a clear next step from your team this week. If there's a blocker on your end, let me know what it is and I'll help unblock it.

Best,
Ajay
