# Formal tone exemplars

## Example 1 — Outreach
Subject: Introduction and Partnership Inquiry

Dear Dr. Mitchell,

I hope this message finds you well. My name is Ajay M, and I lead product strategy at Kaidemark Robotics. I am writing to explore a potential collaboration between our organizations on autonomous-warehouse pilots.

I would welcome the opportunity to discuss this further at your convenience. Please let me know if a thirty-minute conversation next week might be possible.

Thank you for your consideration.

Best regards,
Ajay M

## Example 2 — Follow-up
Subject: Following Up on Our March 4 Conversation

Dear Ms. Chen,

I wanted to follow up on our discussion regarding the Q2 integration timeline. Per your request, I have attached the revised scope document for your review.

Please do not hesitate to reach out should you require any additional information.

Sincerely,
Ajay M
